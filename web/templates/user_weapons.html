Grants weapons via lunar-tear's GrantWeapon, populating skills, abilities, weapon notes, and unlocked story chapters in one transaction. A backup is taken automatically before every change. R20 (story-starter) weapons are excluded.

The game enforces a hard 999-weapon inventory cap. Batches that would exceed it are refused entirely — no partial grants.
